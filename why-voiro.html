Our Story

Founded in 2015, Voiro began with a simple mission: to make enterprise-grade communications accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Our team of VoIP experts, network engineers, and customer support professionals came together with decades of experience in telecommunications. We saw that traditional phone systems were expensive, inflexible, and difficult to manage, while many cloud solutions sacrificed quality for convenience.

Today, Voiro serves thousands of businesses worldwide with reliable, scalable, and cost-effective communication solutions. We've built a global network with Tier-1 carrier interconnections, multiple points of presence, and 24/7 monitoring to ensure your communications never fail.
