SIP trunking is a method of delivering telephone and other communication services over the internet using the Session Initiation Protocol (SIP). It connects your PBX to the Public Switched Telephone Network (PSTN) via the internet, replacing traditional telephone lines. With Voiro's SIP trunking, you get high-quality voice calls, scalability, and significant cost savings compared to traditional phone lines.

Number porting typically takes 7-10 business days, depending on the current carrier and location. We handle the entire porting process for you, including submitting the port request, coordinating with your current provider, and testing the ported numbers. We provide regular updates throughout the process.
